PORTFOLIO AI IMAGE PROMPTS
For Infinix Solutions portfolio visuals

Recommended visual direction
- Format: 4:5 vertical for portfolio cards
- Style: cinematic productized tech illustration
- Mood: premium, strategic, data-driven, high-trust B2B
- Color palette: deep navy, teal, emerald, soft amber accents, graphite, clean white highlights
- Composition: layered dashboards, flowing data paths, subtle holographic UI, modern enterprise environments
- Avoid: cheesy robots, generic stock-office scenes, cartoon icons, low-detail SaaS blobs, cluttered UI, childish AI imagery

Global base prompt
Create a premium editorial-style technology illustration for a modern B2B digital transformation company. The image should feel sophisticated, enterprise-grade, and strategic, with layered data interfaces, cinematic lighting, glassmorphism panels, subtle depth, and realistic digital systems visualization. Clean composition, high contrast, refined typography placeholders, elegant UI fragments, no people posing for camera, no cheesy robots, no generic stock-photo look, no oversaturated neon. Emphasize systems thinking, data flow, automation, and productized technical delivery. 4:5 aspect ratio.

Negative prompt
cartoon, anime, smiling call-center agents, handshake stock photo, cheap 3D render, childish interface, random code screenshots, cyberpunk overload, purple neon spam, blurry text, distorted hands, obvious AI artifacts, watermark, low contrast, flat composition
